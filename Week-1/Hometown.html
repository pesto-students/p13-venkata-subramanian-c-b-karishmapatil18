Dhule,India


Location

Dhule is a city located in the Dhule District in the northwestern part of Maharashtra state, India known as West Khandesh.


About

Situated on the banks of Panzara River, Dhule is the regional headquarters of MIDC, RTO, and MTDC. The city is mainly known for the Hindu temple of Adishakti Ekvira and Swaminarayan Temple
